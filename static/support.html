MealSwipe Support

Need help with MealSwipe, your account, saved recipes, posts, or subscriptions? Contact us and we will help you as soon as possible.

Email support: support@mealswipe.app

Typical response time: 1 to 3 business days.

What We Can Help With

  • Account access and profile questions.
  • Saved recipes, favorites, posts, comments, likes, and follows.
  • Reporting inappropriate content or safety concerns.
  • Subscription access, restore purchases, and premium features.
  • Privacy and account deletion requests.

Subscriptions

MealSwipe subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel your subscription from iOS Settings, Apple ID, Subscriptions. If your premium access does not appear after purchase, use Restore Purchases in the app or contact us.

Account and Privacy Requests

For privacy questions, account deletion, or data requests, email us at support@mealswipe.app.
