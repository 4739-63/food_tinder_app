MealSwipe Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MealSwipe collects, uses, and protects information when you use the MealSwipe mobile app and related services.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account information, such as your email address, profile name, bio, and profile photo.
  • Content you create or upload, such as posts, photos, videos, captions, comments, likes, follows, and reports.
  • Food preferences, saved recipes, favorites, and filter choices used to personalize your experience.
  • Subscription status used to unlock paid features.
  • Technical information needed to operate the app, such as device push notification tokens and basic app activity.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate MealSwipe, create and manage your account, show profiles and posts, personalize food recommendations, save favorites, manage premium access, send notifications you enable, improve app safety, respond to support requests, and maintain the service.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run the app, such as hosting, database, authentication, storage, analytics, payment, and notification providers. Public profile information and content you post may be visible to other MealSwipe users.

User Content

Posts, profile details, comments, likes, and follows may be visible inside the app according to the features you use. You are responsible for the content you choose to share.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription billing are processed by Apple. MealSwipe receives and stores subscription status only as needed to unlock premium features and provide support.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce our terms. You may contact us to request account or data deletion.

Children's Privacy

MealSwipe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can update your profile information in the app, manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings, and contact us to request help with privacy or account deletion.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No online service can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated date above.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact support@mealswipe.app.
